Projects

  • AI Construction Safety Management Platform Development

    This project aims to predict potential accidents based on daily work plans and to build a chatbot model by fine-tuning GPT with construction domain data.

    Duration: March 2023 - February 2024

    Sponsor: National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF)

  • Smart Construction AI Research Center


    This research center explores the application of AI and robotics technology in the construction industry, focusing on three major research topics:
    - Developing autonomous driving technology for robots and a worker-robot interface using augmented reality.
    - Conducting research on on-site environmental information sensing, Multi-Camera monitoring, and the ethical use of AI in construction bidding.
    - Developing a low-cost non-verbal risk perception ability assessment system for construction workers and AI technology for automatic recognition of risk factors in industrial sites based on CCTV footage.

    Duration: June 2022 - December 2023

    Sponsor: Seoul National University

  • Automated Cycle-Time Measurement for NATM-based Tunnel Construction using Deep Neural Network


    NATM, a tunnel construction method, leverages the ground itself as a major load support and involves repeated cycles of various works. Collecting cycle-time is crucial for its construction management. The goal of this research project is to develop an artificial neural network-based NATM cycle-time data collection method.

    Duration: May 2021 - March 2023

    Sponsor: Obayashi Corporation

  • Optimization Model Development for Semiconductor Construction Work Plan Based on Productivity Data


    This research aims to analyze the factors and impacts of productivity degradation in semiconductor construction through actual data and develop a work plan optimization model. The research objectives include:
    - Establishing and managing measurement processes by construction type.
    - Establishing a model for calculating the impact of productivity degradation factors.
    - Developing a work plan optimization model.

    Duration: February 2021 - December 2022

    Sponsor: Samsung Electronics Corporation
